# SASIGLOBAL | Global Geospatial Authority

This context defines the domain of high-precision geospatial engineering, aerial photogrammetry, and territorial management solutions provided by SASIGLOBAL SAS.

## Language

**Geospatial Intelligence**:
The synthesis of location-based data into actionable insights for decision-making.
_Avoid_: GIS data, map data, spatial info

**Photogrammetry**:
The science of making reliable measurements by the use of photographs and specialized software.
_Avoid_: Aerial photos, mapping, drone shots

**Territorial Management**:
The strategic oversight and regulation of land use, property boundaries, and cadastral records.
_Avoid_: Land management, property tracking

**Industrial Solution**:
A specialized engineering module designed to solve a specific industrial or governmental challenge.
_Avoid_: Service, product, feature

**Digital Twin**:
A high-fidelity digital replica of a physical asset, infrastructure, or terrain.
_Avoid_: 3D model, replica, virtual map

## Relationships

- An **Industrial Solution** leverages **Geospatial Intelligence** to deliver value.
- **Photogrammetry** is a primary input for creating **Digital Twins**.
- **Territorial Management** relies on the precision of **Digital Twins** for legal and operational accuracy.

## Example dialogue

> **Engineer:** "Should we present the **Photogrammetry** results directly to the client?"
> **Expert:** "No, we should process them into a **Digital Twin** first to ensure it integrates with their **Territorial Management** system."

## Flagged ambiguities

- "Project" was used to mean both a client engagement and a software module — resolved: "Operational Mission" for client work, "Industrial Solution" for software/tech modules.
